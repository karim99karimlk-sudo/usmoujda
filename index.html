HISTORY

Union Sportive Musulmane Oujda, also known as USMO, is one of the oldest Moroccan clubs. It was founded in 1958 in the heart of the city of Oujda, Morocco. The club’s colors are black and white, and the team is nicknamed “The Stork of the East” by its supporters. USMO currently plays in the Moroccan second division, BOTOLA PRO 2.

The club plays its home matches at the Municipal Stadium, also known as "The old Stadium" which has a capacity of around 7,000 spectators. Which witnessed historic moments for the club, including memorable victories and crucial matches.

The club's academy has produced several prominent names in national and international football. Over the years USMO has built a strong reputation for developing young talent and nurturing future professionals.
